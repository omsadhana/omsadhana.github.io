About

Om Sadhana Central School is the dream school of R.K.Educational Trust. It stands as a majestic culmination of a Mission of Learning with a Vision of Learned Living.Br.R.Kannan, great grandson of ‘KochadaiVallal’ in Divinity K.Muthuthevar is the Founder Chairman of the Trust and the School.It has been founded on a social commitment of disseminating education of supreme Quality affiliated to the "Central Board of Secondary Education,New Delhi".

"Learning is our Mission
              Learned Living is our Vision"

Our Mission

"Learning" is our mission. Infact learning qualifies life. Life of learning is lifelong. Learning is an exclusive quality of man. Mankind sans learning will stand bankrupt knowledge, wisdom and development. Hence Om Sadhana Central School stands distinguished by adopting such a noble mission of Learning.

Our Vision

"Learned Living" is our vision. Any learning acquires life only when it is understood, applied, followed and lived. Infact learned living is a manifestation of effective learning. Learning becomes vibrant and radiant only when it is given interpretation in leading a life enlivened by learning. Learning, without application in life, is left insignificant whereas learning qualified learned living and refines and humanises the entire Mankind. Hence Om Sadhana Central School has acquired an insighted and foresighted vision of Learned Living.

Our Motto : " Unity,Utility,Universality "

Unity : Unity means unifiying of resources. Intrapersonal unity signifies unity of thought, word and deed of an individual. It is the life giving force to integrity of individuals. Interpersonal unity facilitates domestic, communal, religious, social and national unity. Infact Interpersonal Unity activated by Intrapersonal Unity is a vital ingredient of international unity of Brotherhood.

Utility : Utility denotes usefulness-useful to self, family, society, country and humanity at large. Any kind of unity without the value of utility is deemed to be unproductive. So Om Sadhana makes a 'Vow' to generate a vibrant value of useful living in the upcoming generation.

Universality : Atmospheric air and light are the two natural resources universally common to all nations of the world. No nation can stop the flow of air. No nation can pass a resolution to forbid light. Just as light and air which have a universal right of existence and a universal value of utility, a learned man on his Learned Living stands infused with a noble spirit of Universality. Any learned person leading a learned living is endowed with a Universal Vision of Consciousness, Existence and Immortality.
