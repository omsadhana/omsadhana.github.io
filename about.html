About

Om sadhana central school is the dream school of R.K.Educational Trust .It stands as a majestic culmination of a Mission of Learning with a Vision of Learned Living.Br.R.Kannan, great grandson of ‘KochadaiVallal’ in Divinity K.Muthuthevar is the Founder Chairman of the Trust and the School.It has been founded on a social commitment of disseminating education of supreme Quality affiliated to the "Central Board of Secondary Education,New Delhi".
