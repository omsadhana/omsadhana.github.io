Hari Chandra Hall

Learning Brothers and Sisters are given a special self – responsible experience of writing examinations, without invigilation by teachers. They are trained to be Self – disciplined and Honest in writing Examinations without indulging in any kind of malpractice even when there is no supervisor.

Save Power & Save Fuel Day

Thursday is a Power Off day in Sadhana. Every Thursday has yet another glory in Sadhana because every Thursday is a Save Fuel Day also. Learning Brothers & Sisters are motivated either to walk or to cycle to school. In this context parents are also informed by messages to encourage Learning Brothers & Sisters to avoid individual and personal use of power vehicles to school on Thursdays.
